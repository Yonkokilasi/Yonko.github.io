Here you will find extracts to my latest poems Thank you Lord for these words, Thank you Lord for these words, Drip drop
Tick tock
I hear the clock
A new day comes
A new day goes
Time like a river flows
But my addiction constantly in tow
I’m trapped in my mistakes
For I lack
The conviction to stop
Over and over
Again and again
I’m coursing through the pain
Just to satiate what I crave
It all started as a way to pass time
Now time is what passes as I dig my grave
I’ve done all I can
But I can’t seem to shake this
The biggest skeleton in my closet
I build a wall around my door step
Help wanted signs are the only things that seem to hang around long enough
